# Extra Collections

Extra collections are a way for you to group and categorise your extras in a presentational way to make it easier for bookers to navigate and find the right extra for them. You may for example have a number of extras related to camping, such as 'Camping Pitch', 'Campervan Pitch' and 'Caravan Pitch'.

Collections can be nested within each other so you could have a top-level collection for 'Camping' and then nested collections for the various types of camping extra such as 'Pitch', 'Electric Hookups' and 'Marquees' and associate the relevant extras to each type of camping collection.

Other applications for collections might be to separate out different types of extras such as 'Food', 'Team', 'Merchandise' and 'Accommodation' extras. The way you use collections is entirely up to you and how you want to present the extra options to your bookers.
