# Extra Collections

The functionality for managing Extra Collections is identical to that of Ticket Collections.  
For detailed instructions and examples, please refer to the [Ticket Collections documentation](../tickets/ticket-collections.md).

This includes features such as:

- Creating and nesting collections
- Assigning extras to collections
- Reordering collections
- Managing collection capacities

By following the Ticket Collections guide, you can apply the same steps to organize and manage your Extra Collections effectively.
